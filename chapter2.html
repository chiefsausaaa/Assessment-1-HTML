Overview

Valorant is a tactical shooter by Riot Games that combines precise gunplay with unique agent abilities for a competitive and strategic experience.

Developer:Riot Games
Release Date:June 2, 2020
Genre:Tactical Shooter

Agents

  • Jett – Agile duelist.
  • Omen – Master of shadows.
  • Viper – Toxic controller.
  • Cypher – Surveillance expert.
  • Sage – Healing support.
  • Brimstone – Tactical support.
